About Us

Laki Café & Pet Hotel in Melaka is inspired by Kiki & Lala 🐶. We are a plant-friendly, pet-friendly café with separated zones for pet lovers and those who prefer a quiet corner. Upstairs, we provide daycare and a boutique pet hotel.

Services

  • ☕ Café with pet and non-pet zones
  • 🏡 Pet Daycare (11am–8pm)
  • 🛏️ Pet Hotel (private rooms for dogs & cats)
