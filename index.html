About Us

Laki Café & Pet Hotel in Melaka is inspired by Kiki & Lala 🐶🐶 and Lily 🐱. We are a plant-friendly, pet-friendly café with separated zones for pet lovers and those who prefer a quiet corner.

Services

  • ☕ Café with pet and non-pet zones
  • 🏠 Pet Daycare (11am–8pm)
  • 🐾 Pet Hotel (private rooms for dogs & cats)

Rates

🐶 Dog Hotel

  • Standard room (per day): RM45
  • 2 family dogs share (per day): RM75
  • Stay > 5 days: 10% off

🐱 Cat Boarding

  • Single cat: RM35
  • Two cats (same household): RM60
  • Stay > 5 days: 10% off

House Rules

  • Bring vaccination records for pets staying in hotel/daycare.
  • All pets must wear diaper.
  • No aggressive pets for the safety of others.
